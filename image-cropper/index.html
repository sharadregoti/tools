How to Use

  1. Upload an image by dragging and dropping, selecting a file, or pasting from clipboard.
  2. Select an aspect ratio for cropping (optional).
  3. Adjust the crop area by dragging the corners or edges.
  4. Use the rotate buttons if needed.
  5. Click the "Crop Image" button when you're satisfied with the selection.
  6. Download the cropped image.

Tips

  • For precise cropping, zoom in by scrolling with your mouse wheel.
  • You can move the image within the crop box by dragging it.
  • The "Free" aspect ratio option allows you to create a custom-sized crop.
  • Common aspect ratios are provided for convenience (1:1 for square, 16:9 for widescreen, etc.).
  • All processing happens in your browser - your images are never uploaded to a server.
